Empowering Youth Through Basketball

Fostering Nigerian Talent

Welcome to the Oluwatoyin Youth Basketball Foundation, a nurturing ground for the next generation of Nigerian basketball stars.

About Us

Founded by Prince Adekunle Oluwatoyin, our mission is to cultivate basketball talent across Nigeria. We aim to provide a transformative platform for youth engagement, teaching values like teamwork, discipline, and perseverance both on and off the court.

Our foundation is more than a sports program; it's a community where young athletes grow into well-rounded individuals.

Our Programs

Talent Identification and Development

We scout and nurture promising young athletes from all corners of Nigeria, especially focusing on underprivileged communities. Our comprehensive training program covers skill development, physical conditioning, and mental toughness.

Alumni and Impact

Hakeem Olajuwon

A shining example of our success, Hakeem Olajuwon started his journey here and went on to become an NBA legend. His story is a testament to the potential that lies within our program.
